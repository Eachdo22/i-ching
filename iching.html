The I Ching or, Book of Changes

The I Ching (pronounced ee-ching) translates to Book of Changes.
It orginated as a manual for divination about 3,000 years ago during the Western Zhou period (c. 1000-750 BCE).
The book is organized around 78 diagrams:

2 + 4 + 8 + 64

The basis of the diagrams are the Two lines: solid (Yang) and broken (Yin).
These two lines create Four Elements.
The Four Elements build Eight Trigrams.
The Eight Trigrams combine to create 64 Hexagrams.
